# Employee Management

This guide provides an overview of the Employee management system, detailing how to manage employees associated with organizations and agencies. Effective employee management is crucial for optimizing workforce performance and ensuring organizational success. This system allows users to create, retrieve, update, and delete employee information efficiently.

## Overview

Employees can be defined by various attributes such as name, department, role, and their associated organization or agency. The management system enables users to perform the following operations:

- Create new employees.
- Retrieve a list of all employees.
- Get detailed information about a specific employee by ID.
- Update existing employee details.
- Delete employees that are no longer needed.
- Retrieve employees based on their associated organization.
